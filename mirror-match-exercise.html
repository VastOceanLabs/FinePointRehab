Mirror Match Exercise

How It Works

Mirror Match is a bilateral coordination exercise that helps improve communication between both sides of your brain and enhances motor control in both hands.

You'll see circles appear on both the left and right sides of the screen. Your goal is to touch the matching circles using both hands - left hand for left side, right hand for right side.

The exercise tracks patterns that help you develop symmetrical movements and cross-body coordination.

Benefits: Improves bilateral coordination, enhances motor planning, and strengthens neural pathways between brain hemispheres.
