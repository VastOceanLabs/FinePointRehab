Comet Tap Exercise

How to Play

Glowing comets will streak across the screen from different directions. Your goal is to tap or click on each comet before it disappears off the edge of the screen. This exercise helps improve hand-eye coordination, reaction time, and fine motor control.

Tip: Faster reactions and smaller targets earn higher scores!
