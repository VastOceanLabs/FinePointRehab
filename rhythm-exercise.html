How to Play

Watch: The game will light up squares in a pattern on the 3×3 grid.

Remember: Pay attention to the order and sequence of the lights.

Repeat: Touch the squares in the same order you saw them light up.

Advance: Each round adds one more square to remember. See how far you can go!
